OUR ADVOCACY:
The advocacy aims to create a safer online environment for people of all genders and dispositions in the name of upholding respect for all people. It aims to educate individuals and groups who perpetuate harmful ideologies and eradicate gender-based harassment in online spaces.

What are our
OBJECTIVES?

  1. 01

    Give representation to individuals that have insecurities about their gender disposition active social media posting and a public awareness plan.

  2. 02

    Admonishment of harrassment, isolation and derogation of individuals on a gender basis through public materials produced by the advocacy.

  3. 03

    he awareness promotion of laws like the SOGIE bill in order to inform individuals of the rights and defense they have against harassment and isolation online.

  4. 04

    Post and disseminate PubMats on social media regarding on how to properly respect each other when speaking, and how certain words or phrases can negatively affect some people.

  5. 05

    Create dedicated chat threads and online spaces where one is able to talk about their problems openly and freely without harrassment.
